# Hotel Policies - User Guide

## What Are Hotel Policies?

Hotel policies are rules and guidelines that help guests understand your hotel's requirements and offerings. They appear on your public hotel page and during the booking process.

## Available Policies

### Standard Policies

These are pre-defined policies you can enable and customize:

#### 1. **Age Restriction** 👤
Set the minimum age requirement for check-in.
- **Example**: "The minimum age for check-in is 18"
- **Input**: Numeric value (e.g., 18, 21)

#### 2. **Pets** 🐻
Describe your pet policy and any charges.
- **Example**: "Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable."
- **Input**: Text description

#### 3. **Groups** 👥
Explain your policy for group bookings.
- **Example**: "When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply."
- **Input**: Text description

#### 4. **Smoking** 🚭
Specify smoking rules in your hotel.
- **Example**: "Smoking is not allowed in rooms. Smoking area available in the lobby."
- **Input**: Text description

#### 5. **Quiet Hours** 🔇
Set quiet hours for guests.
- **Example**: "Quiet hours from 10 PM to 8 AM. Please keep noise levels low."
- **Input**: Text description

#### 6. **Parties & Events** 🎉
Explain your policy on parties and events.
- **Example**: "Parties and events are not allowed in rooms."
- **Input**: Text description

### Custom Policies

Add any additional policies specific to your hotel:
- Early check-in availability
- Late check-out options
- Parking information
- WiFi details
- Breakfast included
- Gym access
- Pool hours
- Any other house rules

## How to Add/Edit Policies

### Step 1: Go to Hotel Edit
1. Navigate to `/dashboard/listings`
2. Find your hotel
3. Click **"Manage Hotel"** button
4. Click **"Edit Hotel"** button

### Step 2: Find Policies Section
Scroll down to the **"Policies"** section in the edit modal.

### Step 3: Enable Standard Policies
- Check the box next to each policy you want to enable
- Fill in the specific details for that policy
- Leave unchecked if you don't want to display that policy

### Step 4: Add Custom Policies (Optional)
1. Click **"Add Policy"** button
2. Enter policy title (e.g., "Early Check-in Available")
3. Enter policy description (e.g., "Available for $25 per hour")
4. Click **"Add"**

### Step 5: Save Changes
1. Scroll to bottom of modal
2. Click **"Save Changes"**
3. Wait for success message
4. Modal closes automatically

## Where Guests See Your Policies

### 1. Hotel Details Page
When guests view your hotel (`/stay/{hotelId}`), they see:
- All enabled policies with icons
- Policy descriptions
- Specific details (e.g., minimum age)

### 2. Booking Confirmation
Before confirming their booking, guests see:
- All applicable policies
- Important rules they need to follow

## Tips for Writing Good Policies

### Be Clear and Specific
❌ Bad: "We have rules"
✅ Good: "Quiet hours from 10 PM to 8 AM. Please keep noise levels low."

### Be Honest
❌ Bad: "Pets allowed" (but you charge $50/night)
✅ Good: "Pets allowed for $50 per night"

### Be Helpful
❌ Bad: "No early check-in"
✅ Good: "Early check-in available for $25 per hour, subject to availability"

### Use Simple Language
❌ Bad: "Guests must adhere to our noise ordinance regulations"
✅ Good: "Please keep noise levels low after 10 PM"

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I added a policy but guests don't see it**
- Make sure the policy is checked/enabled
- Refresh the hotel page
- Check that you clicked "Save Changes"

**Q: Can I edit a policy after saving?**
- Yes! Just click "Edit Hotel" again and make changes

**Q: Can I delete a policy?**
- For standard policies: uncheck the box
- For custom policies: click the delete button

**Q: How many policies can I add?**
- Unlimited! Add as many as you need

**Q: Do policies affect booking?**
- No, policies are informational only
- They help guests understand your rules before booking

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it concise** - Guests don't want to read a novel
2. **Be transparent** - Disclose all charges and restrictions upfront
3. **Update regularly** - Keep policies current with your actual practices
4. **Be consistent** - Apply policies fairly to all guests
5. **Highlight positives** - Use custom policies to showcase amenities
